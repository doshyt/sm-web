Security and Privacy


Data Security and Processing


Whistle Willow is designed with security and privacy at the core of product by the team with the professional background in security and privacy engineering.
We deliberately opted out of all personal data storage and processing to make Whistle Willow adoption simple and safe.

By default, Whistle Willow does not send data to any third party APIs, does not track users nor use data for product improvement. Your data stays yours, and does not leave Atlassian ecosystem.

Data Privacy


Whistle Willow app does not store personal data in its dedicated storage within Jira platform, as part of out committment to protecting the identity of reporters and privacy standards.
