Breakdown work with Machine Learning!


Maister is a pro in breaking down Epics, Stories, Tasks and Bugs. Its NLP algorithm analyzes all aspects of the issues and makes AI-powered suggestions how to make them crisp and sharp.

Auto-discover blockers and roadbumps


Maister learns if a team is struggling even before the team. Its smart sensors are always tuned to follow issue progression dynamics and spot scope creep, external dependencies or lack of communication. Sometimes, one small poke can save hours of team's work.

Provide AI-generated inputs for retrospectives


Maister has only one goal - to improve development processes. It participates in dev, spots and remembers all opportunities for making things better. Retrospectives are Maister's bread and butter - it is always ready to share!
